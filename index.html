The Big Picture

How Chipotle Calories Add Up

Most people underestimate how quickly calories build at Chipotle. A plain burrito bowl base starts at zero calories. Add chicken, rice, beans, salsa, cheese, sour cream, and guacamole, and you are looking at well over 800 calories — before you even touch the chips.

The flour tortilla used in a burrito is one of the biggest culprits. On its own, it carries around 320 calories and nearly 50 grams of carbohydrates. Switching from a burrito to a bowl instantly saves hundreds of calories before changing a single topping.

The good news is Chipotle gives you full control. You can build a meal anywhere from 350 to 1,200 calories depending on your choices. The key is knowing which ingredients carry the most weight.

Smart Ordering

Pro Tips for Fitness Goals

These strategies help you hit your protein targets, cut unnecessary calories, and still enjoy every bite.

🍗

Double Your Protein

Ordering double chicken bumps your meal to 360 calories and 64 grams of protein — more than most protein shakes for a comparable calorie cost.

🥣

Bowl Over Burrito

Skipping the flour tortilla instantly saves 320 calories and 49 grams of carbohydrates — calories you can invest in extra protein instead.

🫘

Add Black Beans

Beans provide 8 grams of plant-based protein and are loaded with fiber — keeping you fuller for longer at just 130 calories per serving.

🌾

Brown vs. White Rice

Both are 210 calories, but brown rice offers more fiber and a lower glycemic impact — a small swap with a meaningful payoff.

🍅

Go Wild on Salsas

Fresh tomato salsa and green tomatillo are practically calorie-free. Load them on freely for bold flavor without the fat or carbs.

🚫

Skip the Chips

At 540 calories per side, chips can nearly double your meal's calorie count. Skip them or share if you need that crunch.

Customized For You

Chipotle for Every Diet Goal

No matter your eating style, there is a Chipotle build that works perfectly for you.

Keto Friendly

Keto Bowl

Skip all rice and beans. Build around steak or chicken with extra guacamole, cheese, and fresh salsas. Keeps net carbs under 10 grams while delivering healthy fats and protein.

Plant Based

Vegan Bowl

Sofritas make an excellent protein base. Combine with brown rice, black beans, fajita vegetables, and corn salsa for a satisfying plant-based bowl around 600 calories.

Weight Loss

Lean Salad Bowl

A salad bowl with chicken, fajita vegetables, and one or two salsas keeps your meal under 450 calories while delivering enough protein to stay full until your next meal.
